<mark style="color:purple;">They are successful lawyers, civil rights activists, M\&A lawyers, Intellectual Property lawyers, writers, filmmakers, musicians or artists.</mark>

**Consideration:**&#x20;

5\. Appointment to this Legal Council is pro bono and voluntary in nature. Appointments are generally made for an election period of atleast 1 year and atmost 3 years.&#x20;

**Appointment, Nomination, Removal and Resignation:**&#x20;

**Appointment:**&#x20;

6.1 **Nomination:** Following members can officially nominate an existing member of the Foundation to the Legal Council:&#x20;

6.1 a. An elected member of the [Executive Council](../executive-council.md) or of the [Advisory Council](../advisory-council.md) or of the Legal Council.

6.2 Self nomination by (an already elected) member of one of the bodies of the Open Council for his/her/their appointment to the Legal Council is not acceptable.

Self nomination by an existing non Council member of the Foundation, for his/her/their appointment to the Legal Council is not acceptable. [Please read more](../../open-governance/peer-review.md).\
\
6.3 Once the nomination is accepted by the Nominee, Nominee or the member nominating the nominee, shall file supporting information such as nominee's literature, publications, demonstration of social activities on Foundation's Membership Registry.&#x20;
